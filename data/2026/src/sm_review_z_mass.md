# Z boson mass — PDG 2026 Electroweak review (preview)

The 2026 *Standard Model and Constraints on New Physics* review is the first
2026 PDG chapter to publicly document the Z mass world average.

- Source: PDG *Review of Particle Physics* 2026, review "Electroweak Model and
  Constraints on New Physics" (Section 10), authors J. de Blas, S. Dittmaier,
  R. Kogler.
- Downloaded from https://pdg.lbl.gov/2026/reviews/rpp2026-rev-standard-model.pdf
  on 2026-05-15. Review dated **15 April 2026**.
- The PDF is copyrighted and **not** stored; only the extracted values and the
  quoted lines below are kept.

## Headline

**m_Z = 91.1880 ± 0.0020 GeV** (world average used in the SM fit, eq. 10.29 —
LEP-1 combination plus the CDF MZ result; unchanged from PDG 2025).

The LEP-1-only result is m_Z = 91.1876 ± 0.0021 GeV (eq. 10.28).

## New hadron-collider measurements (not yet folded into the average)

| experiment | m_Z (GeV) | status |
|---|---:|---|
| LEP-1 (ALEPH+DELPHI+L3+OPAL) | 91.1876 ± 0.0021 | dominant input |
| CDF (dimuon) | 91.1920 ± 0.0075 | in average (combined with LEP-1 → 91.1880 ± 0.0020) |
| CDF (dielectron) | 91.1943 ± 0.0158 | noted |
| CMS (Z → μ⁺μ⁻) | 91.1858 ± 0.0048 | flagged, not independent of LEP-1 (calibration) |
| LHCb (Z → μ⁺μ⁻, 2024) | 91.1842 ± 0.0093 | "will be included in future combinations" |
| ATLAS | 91.1920 ± 0.0075 | flagged |

## Z width

Γ_Z = 2.4955 ± 0.0023 GeV (LEP-1 final, eq. 10.30 — also unchanged from PDG 2025).
